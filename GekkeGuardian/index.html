How It Works

Simple, fast, and accurate

Gekke Guardian analyzes content using multiple AI models that each provide a confidence score. If the score exceeds your configured threshold, the content gets flagged, it's that simple.

1

Multi-Model Analysis

Different AI models analyze almost every frame of media, each giving a confidence score on whether content is NSFW.

2

Threshold Check

If the confidence score exceeds your threshold, the content gets flagged. You control how strict it is.

3

Link Inspection

For links, it fetches the website's data and uses AI to determine if it's an adult site before anyone clicks.

Setup Guide

Get Gekke Guardian running in 3 steps
1

Invite the Bot

Add Gekke Guardian to your Discord server. It needs permissions to manage messages and view channels.

Add to Server
2

Create & Configure a List

Click New List on this dashboard, then adjust your moderation settings.

Don't forget to press Save & Exit at the bottom of the edit page after configuring.
3

Link Your List in Discord

Inside your Discord server, run this slash command:

/link

The bot will ask for your List ID. Copy it from your dashboard card and paste it in Discord.

You're all set! The bot will now actively moderate using your configured settings.
